Kimchi Stew

Kimchi stew is a korean dish, made with kimchi and other ingredients, such as seafood, scallions, onions, and diced dubu. It is one of famous stews in Korean cuisine.

Gimbap

MRP ₹ 259.00

Gimbap is a Korean dish made from cooked rice and different vegetables rolled in gim—dried sheets of seaweed—and served in bite-sized slices.

Bibimbap

Bibimbap simply translates to “mixed rice with assorted vegetables“. You can make endless variations to dish depending on preference.

Tteokbokki

Tteokbokki (떡볶이), or simmered rice cake, is a popular Korean food made from small-sized, white, cylinder-shaped rice cakes called tteokmyeon.

Ramen

Ramen is a Japanese noodle dish. It consists of wheat noodles served in a broth; common flavors are soy sauce and miso, with typical toppings.

Jajangmyeon

Fried Zucuni

Coleslaw

Taiyaki

MRP ₹ 99.00

Doracake
